Frequently Asked Questions About Before and After Care

What are your hours of operation and do you offer before/after care?
Our preschool operates Monday through Friday from 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM. Our core preschool program runs from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM, with before-care available starting at 6:30 AM and after-care until 6:00 PM at no additional cost. We understand many Marysville families commute to Boeing, Seattle, or Everett, so our extended hours are designed to accommodate working parents.
What are your current tuition rates and what's included?
Full-time preschool tuition (M-F) is $1,275 per month, which includes before/after care, all meals (breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snack), and our full educational curriculum. Part-time options are available at $875/month for 3 days per week. Summer camp programs run June through August and are $325 per week. We offer a 10% sibling discount and accept DSHS subsidies for qualifying families.
What age groups do you accept and what are your teacher-to-student ratios?
We accept children ages 2.5 to 5 years for our preschool program, and ages 5-12 for our summer camp. Our teacher-to-student ratios exceed state requirements: 1:7 for our younger preschoolers (ages 2.5-3), 1:10 for pre-K (ages 4-5), and 1:12 for summer camp. All lead teachers have Early Childhood Education degrees or equivalent certification, as required by Washington state licensing.
How do I enroll my child and is there a waiting list?
To enroll, first schedule a tour of our facility by calling us or using our online booking system. After the tour, you can complete our online registration form and pay a $150 non-refundable registration fee to secure your spot. Currently, we have limited openings in our pre-K program, but our 2.5-3 year old class has a 3-4 month waiting list. We recommend getting on our waiting list early, as spots fill quickly, especially for fall enrollment.
What safety protocols and licensing does your facility maintain?
We are fully licensed by the Washington State Department of Children, Youth, and Families (DCYF) and maintain all required safety certifications. Our facility features secure entry with keypad access, all staff are background-checked and First Aid/CPR certified, and we conduct monthly emergency drills. We're located in a purpose-built facility in Forest Park with fenced outdoor play areas, and we follow strict health protocols including daily sanitization and illness policies. Our license is prominently displayed in our lobby, and our last inspection report is available upon request.
