Frequently Asked Questions About Half-Day Preschool Program

What are your preschool hours and program options?
We offer half-day programs running Monday through Friday with two schedule options: Morning Session (8:30 AM - 11:30 AM) or Afternoon Session (12:30 PM - 3:30 PM). Families can choose between 2-day (T/Th), 3-day (M/W/F), or 5-day programs. Our summer camp program runs in July and August with flexible weekly enrollment options.
What are your current tuition rates for the preschool program?
Our 2023-24 monthly tuition rates are: 2-day program: $295/month, 3-day program: $395/month, and 5-day program: $595/month. We require a $150 non-refundable registration fee and first month's tuition to secure your child's spot. We offer a 10% sibling discount for families with multiple children enrolled.
What age groups do you accept and what are your teacher-to-student ratios?
We accept children ages 2.5 to 5 years old. Our classes are organized by age: Junior Preschool (2.5-3.5 years) and Senior Preschool (3.5-5 years). We maintain a 1:7 teacher-to-student ratio in our Junior Preschool and 1:10 ratio in our Senior Preschool, exceeding Washington state licensing requirements. Maximum class sizes are 14 students for Junior and 20 for Senior classes.
Are you licensed and what are your teachers' qualifications?
Yes, we are fully licensed by the Washington State Department of Children, Youth, and Families (DCYF). All our lead teachers hold degrees in Early Childhood Education or related fields and have a minimum of 3 years teaching experience. Our staff members are CPR/First Aid certified and complete ongoing professional development throughout the year. We also undergo annual health and safety inspections.
What is your enrollment process and is there a waitlist?
Start by scheduling a tour of our facility in Lynnwood by calling or emailing us. After the tour, you can complete our online enrollment application. Currently enrolled families have priority registration in January for the following school year, then we open registration to the public in February. We do maintain a waitlist when classes are full, and spots are offered in the order applications are received. A $50 waitlist fee is required, which is applied to your registration fee if a spot becomes available.
